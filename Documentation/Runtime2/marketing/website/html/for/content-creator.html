Publish everywhere from
one workflow.

You create one piece of content, then spend an hour distributing it. Blog. Medium. Social posts. Newsletter. Content calendar. Analytics check. The creating takes 2 hours. The publishing and tracking takes another 2. And half the time you forget one platform.

The PLang way

Start.goal
Start- start webserver- every day at 8am, call !CheckContentCalendar
PublishPost - POST- insert into posts, title=%request.title%, body=%request.body%,    status='published', publishedAt=%Now%, write to %postId%- call !DistributePost- write out {postId: %postId%, status: 'published and distributed'}
DistributePost- post https://api.medium.com/v1/users/me/posts    data={title: '%request.title%', content: '%request.body%',    contentFormat: 'markdown', publishStatus: 'public'}    write to %mediumResult%- post https://api.twitter.com/2/tweets    data={text: 'New post: %request.title% - Read more at https://myblog.com/posts/%postId%'}    write to %tweetResult%- insert into distribution_log, postId=%postId%,    medium=%mediumResult.id%, twitter=%tweetResult.id%, date=%Now%
CheckContentCalendar- select * from content_calendar    where dueDate=%Tomorrow% and status='planned', write to %dueTomorrow%- foreach %dueTomorrow%, call !RemindToWrite item=%item%
RemindToWrite- send email to me@creator.com,    subject "Content Due Tomorrow: %item.title%",    body "Your planned content '%item.title%' is due tomorrow. Notes: %item.notes%"
AddToCalendar - POST- insert into content_calendar, title=%request.title%,    notes=%request.notes%, dueDate=%request.dueDate%, status='planned'- write out {status: 'added to calendar'}

That's your entire publishing workflow.

Publish once, distribute to Medium and Twitter, content calendar with daily reminders, analytics tracking. One file. Want to add LinkedIn? Add one more line.

What just happened

  • post https://api.medium.comCross-post to Medium automatically on every publish.
  • post https://api.twitter.comTweet the link automatically. No manual copy-paste.
  • insert into distribution_logTrack where each post was distributed. Full audit trail.
  • every day at 8amContent calendar checked daily. Reminders for tomorrow's deadlines.
  • insert into content_calendarYour content calendar lives in a database, not a spreadsheet.
  • send emailDeadline reminders and reports delivered to your inbox.

Your publishing workflow adapts as your distribution grows. Add a platform, change reminder timing, add a weekly analytics email — change one line each time.

The build / run split

PLang uses AI at build time to compile your English into executable instructions. At runtime, no AI — your publishing workflow runs reliably with zero ongoing cost. Build once, your content distribution runs itself.

Get started
