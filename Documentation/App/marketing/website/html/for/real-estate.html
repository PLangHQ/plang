Listings and follow-ups
on autopilot.

15 active listings, 40 prospects, a phone that never stops. Between showings, you're manually updating listings, sending follow-up emails to prospects who showed interest three days ago, trying to remember who wanted a 3-bedroom with a garage under $400k. That prospect who was "very interested" last Tuesday? You forgot to call them back.

The PLang way

Start.goal
Start- start webserver- every day at 8am, call !DailyFollowUps
AddProspect - POST- insert into prospects, name=%request.name%,    email=%request.email%, phone=%request.phone%,    bedrooms=%request.bedrooms%, budget=%request.budget%,    features=%request.features%, lastContact=%Now%,    status='active', write to %id%- write out {prospectId: %id%, status: 'added'}
DailyFollowUps- select * from prospects where status='active'    and lastContact <= %Now-3days%, write to %dueProspects%- foreach %dueProspects%, call !FollowUpProspect item=%prospect%
FollowUpProspect- select * from listings where bedrooms >= %prospect.bedrooms%    and price <= %prospect.budget% and status='active',    write to %matches%- send email to %prospect.email%,    subject "New listings matching your criteria",    body "Hi %prospect.name%, here are properties that match what you're looking for:\n\n%matches%\n\nLet me know if you'd like to schedule a showing."- update prospects set lastContact=%Now% where id=%prospect.id%
AddListing - POST- insert into listings, address=%request.address%,    price=%request.price%, bedrooms=%request.bedrooms%,    features=%request.features%, status='active',    listed=%Now%, write to %id%- write out {listingId: %id%, status: 'listed'}
WeeklyReport- select count(*) as active from listings where status='active',    write to %listingCount%- select count(*) as active from prospects where status='active',    write to %prospectCount%- select count(*) as sent from prospects where lastContact >= %Now-7days%,    write to %followUpCount%- send email to agent@realty.com,    subject "Weekly Pipeline Report",    body "Active listings: %listingCount.active%\nActive prospects: %prospectCount.active%\nFollow-ups sent this week: %followUpCount.sent%"

That's your prospect management system.

Automatic follow-ups, property matching, email notifications, weekly reports. It describes exactly what it does.

What just happened

  • insert into prospects/listingsProspect and listing databases created automatically. No CRM subscription.
  • every day at 8amFollow-ups sent before your first showing of the day. No prospect forgotten.
  • select from listings where price <= budgetAutomatic property matching. Prospects get listings that fit their criteria.
  • send emailPersonalized emails with matching properties. Professional and automatic.
  • AddProspect/AddListing - POSTAPI endpoints for web forms. Capture leads from your website directly.

Need to follow up every 5 days instead of 3? Change one line. Need to match on garage or pool? Add a column.

The build / run split

PLang uses AI at build time to compile your English into executable instructions. At runtime, no AI — your prospect system runs reliably with zero ongoing cost. Build once, your follow-ups and matching run forever.
